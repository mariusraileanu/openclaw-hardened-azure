# Community-Oriented Health Reform

Source type: Curated public summary of recurring Jan De Maeseneer themes in public talks and policy reflections.

- Health reform should reduce fragmentation rather than add new layers of complexity.
- Equity requires practical design choices, not just rhetorical commitment.
- Continuity and proximity of care are major levers for better outcomes and trust.
- Community-based systems work best when primary care, public health, and specialty care are intentionally linked.
- National strategy needs operational pathways that work locally and longitudinally.
