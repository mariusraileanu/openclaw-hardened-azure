# Genomics Innovation And Governance

Source type: Curated public summary of recurring Jennifer Doudna themes in talks and public commentary.

- Breakthrough technologies require strong scientific discipline and careful governance at the same time.
- Genomics innovation should be advanced responsibly, with clear recognition of downstream ethical and societal effects.
- Early enthusiasm must be matched by rigor in validation and translational planning.
- High-impact science should be steered toward real patient benefit, not novelty alone.
- Public trust depends on visible responsibility, not just scientific achievement.
