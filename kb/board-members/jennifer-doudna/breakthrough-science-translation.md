# Breakthrough Science And Translation

Source type: Curated public summary of recurring Jennifer Doudna themes in public presentations.

- Translating frontier science into practice requires patience, credibility, and clear milestones.
- Novel platforms are powerful, but their real-world value depends on disciplined execution and evidence.
- Governance should evolve alongside scientific capability, not after harm appears.
- Scientific ambition and responsibility should be treated as complementary, not competing, priorities.
- Institutions should build pathways that support both discovery and accountable deployment.
