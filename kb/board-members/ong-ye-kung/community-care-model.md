# Community-Based Care And Population Health

Source type: Curated public summary of recurring Ong Ye Kung themes in speeches and public commentary.

- Primary care should serve as the anchor of a more integrated and preventive health system.
- Community-based care models need practical implementation support, not just policy endorsement.
- Health reform should improve both access and resilience over time.
- Delivery design must fit real population needs across diverse care settings.
- Policy ambition should be matched by accountable execution.
