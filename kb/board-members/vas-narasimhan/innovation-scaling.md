# Innovation Scaling And Portfolio Discipline

Source type: Curated public summary of recurring Vas Narasimhan themes in public talks.

- Organizations need disciplined prioritization to turn promising science into real-world impact.
- Scale requires focusing resources on pathways with clear evidence and strategic coherence.
- Partnerships and platform thinking matter when bringing innovation into broad use.
- Strong innovation systems combine scientific ambition with operational accountability.
- Commercial success should follow credible development logic, not replace it.
