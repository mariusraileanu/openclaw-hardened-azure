# Cultural Fit And Complementary Care

Source type: Curated public summary of recurring Huang Luqi themes in public talks and commentary.

- Cultural fit and patient acceptance are important design considerations in integrative care.
- Complementary approaches can support patient-centered systems when roles and limits are clear.
- Integration should strengthen care pathways rather than add confusion or inconsistency.
- Traditional modalities should be situated within explicit governance and referral logic.
- Reform should consider both scientific standards and the practical cultural realities of care delivery.
