# Integrative Care And Evidence Awareness

Source type: Curated public summary of recurring Huang Luqi themes in public commentary and institutional messaging.

- Integrative care should connect traditional approaches to modern systems in a disciplined and evidence-aware way.
- Complementary modalities should be evaluated for appropriateness, safety, and fit in the broader pathway.
- Evidence standards matter even when approaches come from longstanding traditions.
- Health systems should avoid both dismissal of useful complementary approaches and uncritical adoption.
- Governance is necessary when integrating modalities across diverse care settings.
