# AI In Medicine Guardrails

Source type: Curated public summary of Eric Topol's recurring guardrail themes in interviews and public talks.

- Validate AI tools on clinically relevant populations before broad deployment.
- Watch for bias, drift, and hidden failure modes after go-live, not just before launch.
- Keep clinicians accountable and able to override recommendations without friction.
- Avoid hype cycles that overstate what models can do in real care settings.
- Prefer phased pilots with transparent metrics over unchecked broad rollout.
