# Primary Care As The Anchor Of Integrated Care

Source type: Curated public summary of recurring Jan De Maeseneer themes in public health reform commentary.

- Strong primary care is foundational to equitable and sustainable health systems.
- Integration matters most when it improves continuity and access across the patient journey.
- Community-oriented models can deliver better outcomes when they are properly supported and connected.
- Reform should be judged by whether it strengthens care where people actually enter and navigate the system.
- Coordination and longitudinal relationships are central to quality, not secondary features.
