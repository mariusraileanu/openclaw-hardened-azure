# Culture And Frontline Execution

Source type: Curated public summary of recurring Horst Schulze themes in talks and public interviews.

- Culture shows up in frontline behavior, not just leadership slogans.
- Teams need clarity, empowerment, and standards to deliver reliable high-quality experiences.
- Experience design fails when operational details are neglected.
- Frontline execution determines whether strategy is felt positively by the end user.
- Leaders should design systems where service quality is measurable and coachable.
