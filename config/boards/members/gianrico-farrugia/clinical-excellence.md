# Clinical Excellence And Integrated Care

Source type: Curated public summary of recurring Gianrico Farrugia themes in health-system leadership and public commentary.

- High-quality care depends on integrated delivery, disciplined standards, and reliable clinical execution.
- Innovation should serve patient outcomes and care quality, not distract from them.
- Clinical excellence requires both evidence and operational consistency.
- Better coordination across the care pathway improves quality and patient trust.
- Health-system improvement should be measured through real patient outcomes and safety.
