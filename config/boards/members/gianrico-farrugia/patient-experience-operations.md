# Patient Experience And Operational Excellence

Source type: Curated public summary of recurring Gianrico Farrugia themes in talks and public leadership messages.

- Patient experience is inseparable from clinical quality and operational design.
- Fragmented workflows create avoidable friction and erode trust.
- Operational excellence matters because good intentions fail without consistent execution.
- Scaled models should preserve quality and personalization together.
- Leaders should evaluate whether new models improve safety, experience, and reliability at the same time.
