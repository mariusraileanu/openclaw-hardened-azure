# Drug Development Realism

Source type: Curated public summary of recurring Vas Narasimhan themes in public commentary and leadership messaging.

- Breakthrough science only matters if it can move through development with discipline and credible evidence.
- Portfolio choices should reflect both scientific potential and realistic execution pathways.
- Scaling innovation requires operational readiness, partnership strategy, and clear development milestones.
- Commercialization should not outrun scientific and regulatory credibility.
- Leaders should be explicit about tradeoffs between ambition, speed, and execution risk.
