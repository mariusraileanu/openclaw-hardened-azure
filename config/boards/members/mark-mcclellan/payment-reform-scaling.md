# Payment Reform And Scaling Innovation

Source type: Curated public summary of recurring Mark McClellan themes in policy talks and public commentary.

- National pilots need clear comparators, governance, and decision points for scale-up or shutdown.
- Outcome measures should be attributable, auditable, and understandable to providers.
- Case-mix and population complexity must be accounted for to avoid punishing harder settings.
- Innovation can be supported when contracts preserve flexibility and accountability together.
- Policy success depends on execution details, not just high-level strategy.
