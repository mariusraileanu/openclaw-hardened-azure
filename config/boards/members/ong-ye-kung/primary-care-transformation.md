# Primary Care Transformation And Healthier Living

Source type: Curated public summary of recurring Ong Ye Kung themes in public health leadership and reform.

- Sustainable system reform depends on shifting more care upstream into prevention and primary care.
- Community-based models matter when they reduce downstream hospital dependency and improve continuity.
- Reform must be operationally grounded, not just strategy on paper.
- Population health gains require sustained behavior change, not one-off interventions.
- National reform succeeds when delivery models and incentives reinforce each other.
